1. Who we are

Jiive ("Jiive", "we", "our", "us") is a wellness service that calculates your biological age from blood-test biomarkers and provides AI-assisted health guidance over WhatsApp. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

We are the Data Fiduciary for the personal data described in this policy. By using Jiive (including by sending a WhatsApp message to our service), you consent to the data handling described here.

3. How we use your data

  • To deliver the service — book blood tests, calculate biological age, send results, answer your questions.
  • To personalise — Jiive's AI agent ("Lumi") uses your prior conversations and saved preferences to give relevant responses.
  • To process payments — generate Razorpay payment links and confirm successful payments.
  • To send essential notifications — fasting reminders before your appointment, payment confirmations, results-ready alerts.
  • To improve our AI — anonymised conversation samples may be used to evaluate response quality. We do not use identifiable health data for model training.
  • To comply with law — respond to lawful requests from authorities, prevent fraud, enforce our Terms.

We do not sell your personal data to anyone. We do not use your data for advertising or behavioural profiling.

4. Who we share data with

We share the minimum data necessary with trusted third-party service providers ("Data Processors") to deliver the service. These providers fall into the following categories:

  • Diagnostic lab partners — to dispatch phlebotomists and process blood samples. Your phone number is masked using a relay number; the lab does not receive your real contact details.
  • Payment processors — to generate payment links and confirm transactions. Card, UPI, and banking credentials are collected directly by the processor; we do not see or store them.
  • Messaging platform providers — to deliver and receive messages between you and Jiive over WhatsApp.
  • AI / large-language-model providers — to generate conversational responses and personalised guidance.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers — for hosting, storage, and backups, with data encrypted at rest in India-region data centres.

Each provider is bound by contract to use your data only for the stated purpose and to maintain reasonable security safeguards. We do not sell or rent your data to anyone.

5. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in AWS data centres located in Mumbai, India (ap-south-1). Some processing (LLM inference, WhatsApp routing) may occur in regions outside India operated by our partners; in those cases data is transmitted in encrypted form and we maintain contractual safeguards required under the DPDP Act for cross-border transfers.

6. How long we keep data

  • Identity + service data — for as long as your Jiive account is active, plus up to 12 months after your last interaction.
  • Health results — retained indefinitely while your account is active so you can view your biological-age history; deleted on account deletion.
  • Conversation history — most recent 90 days are retained for context; older messages are pruned automatically.
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting laws.
  • System logs — 30 days, used for security and debugging only.

7. Your rights under the DPDP Act

You have the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — fix inaccurate data (you can also do this by messaging Lumi).
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data (see Section 8).
  • Right to grievance redressal — raise concerns about how we process your data.
  • Right to nominate — designate someone to exercise your rights in case of incapacity or death.
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, with effect going forward.

To exercise any right, contact us using the details in Section 10. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Account & data deletion

To delete your account and associated data:

  1. Send a WhatsApp message to Jiive saying "delete my account", or
  2. Email jiivedevop@jiive.ai from the email or phone number associated with your account.

We will delete your identity data, health results, and conversation history within 30 days. Payment records may be retained for the legally required period (Section 6).

9. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard safeguards: TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest in our database, access controls limiting employee access to data on a need-to-know basis, signed webhook verification (HMAC-SHA256), and regular security reviews. No system is perfectly secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

10. Children

Jiive is intended for adults aged 18 and above. Our system enforces this minimum during sign-up. If we discover we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it.
