Jiive — Privacy Policy

Effective: 1 May 2026  |  Last updated: 1 May 2026

1. About Jiive

Jiive is a wellness service that helps you understand your biological age through blood-test biomarkers, delivered via WhatsApp. This policy explains what personal data we handle and your rights under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). By using Jiive, you consent to the practices described here.

2. What we collect

  • Identity — your WhatsApp number, name, date of birth, gender.
  • Health data — blood-biomarker values, biological-age results, and reports you upload.
  • Service details — pincode and address used for at-home sample collection.
  • Conversations — messages exchanged with our AI assistant.
  • Payment status — transaction confirmations only. We do not store payment instrument details.

3. How we use it

  • To provide the service — bookings, results, AI guidance, reminders.
  • To personalise your experience.
  • To improve service quality and prevent abuse.
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising.

4. Sharing

We share the minimum data necessary with trusted service providers to operate the service — including diagnostic lab partners, payment processors, messaging platforms, AI providers, and cloud infrastructure. All providers are bound by confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Where data is stored

Your data is stored in India, encrypted at rest. Some processing may occur with global service providers under appropriate safeguards.

6. How long we keep it

We retain your data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward. Records required by law (e.g., tax records) are retained for the legally mandated period. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your rights

Under the DPDP Act you have the right to access, correct, or delete your data; to withdraw consent; to nominate someone to act on your behalf; and to raise grievances. To exercise any right, contact us at the email below. We respond within 30 days.

8. Account deletion

To delete your account, message Jiive on WhatsApp saying "delete my account" or email us. We will erase your data within 30 days, except for records we are legally required to retain.

9. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest, and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

10. Age

Jiive is for users aged 18 and above.
